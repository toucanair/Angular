Jonathan Navas

Founder & CEO

Jonathan “ChaChi” Navas founded Toucan Air in 2015, With over a decade of experience, he was determined to create a company dedicated to aviation with the outmost integrity, where customers could really enjoy the rewards of quality, and honesty. Today those core principles are still forged into the core of the business and his employees. While earning his way through college, Jonathan began his aviation career working with Blue Star Aerospace as a shipping and receiving clerk and ended up as warehouse manager. After graduating with a BA in Criminal Justice, he already had the “aviation bug” and his passion for aviation only grew. He took on the role of sales manager for Shoreline Aerospace and from there his career took off, gradually taking on more demanding roles within some of the most reputable companies in Aviation. As the team leader, Jonathan’s vision is to make Toucan Air a true world leader in Aviation. With the help and wisdom of his father and a talented team, Toucan Air continues to grow each day, getting one step closer towards that vision.
During his spare time Jonathan enjoys Scuba diving and BBQing pool side with his family.
“The passion for aviation runs through my veins. That is why I couldn’t imagine myself doing anything else, I have an unfathomable devotion for this industry. I believe we are just beginning to scratch the surface of what aviation and aerospace will be in the future and I want Toucan Air to play a crucial part of this inevitable growth. This is why 4 years ago I set myself on a mission to start a company not only with competitive prices, but a company everyone can trust, with the outmost integrity and unmatched commitment.”
